# ZenTube Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-18_

ZenTube does not collect, transmit, or sell any user data.

## What ZenTube does

- All settings are stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage`.
- No external servers are ever contacted by the extension.
- No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking, no fingerprinting.
- The extension only reads and modifies pages on `youtube.com` for the sole
  purpose of applying your chosen hide rules, focus modes, schedules, and
  Pomodoro overlays.

## Chrome Sync

If you have Chrome Sync enabled on your Google account, your ZenTube settings
will be stored in `chrome.storage.sync` so they follow you across devices.
This data is encrypted and managed by Google; ZenTube has no access to it
outside your own logged-in browser sessions.

## What is stored locally

- Your chosen focus mode and individual hide toggles
- Schedule rules you create (days, times, names)
- Time-limit setting and daily watch-time counter
- Pomodoro configuration and current phase
- Channel whitelist / blacklist names
- "Intentions" you type into the mindful pause (kept in your browser only,
  visible to you in the Stats tab)

## What is NOT collected

- No personally identifiable information
- No browsing history outside of `youtube.com`
- No video titles, channel names you watch, or comment data
- No contact info, no email addresses, no IP logging
- Nothing is ever transmitted off your device by the extension

## Donations

If you choose to donate via Ko-fi or GitHub Sponsors, those services have
their own privacy policies, which apply when you visit their pages. ZenTube
itself receives no payment data.
